Our Story

Our brand name, Amoobo, is inspired by our three beloved dogs: Moomoo, ArB, and Bo. They are an integral part of our family and the source of so much warmth in our lives. We hope our candles can bring that same warmth into your home, helping you enjoy every moment spent there.

Amoobo is a small, family-run candle maker based in Calgary. Every candle is handcrafted with care, from selecting the perfect wax and fragrance to pairing colors with scents. Our goal is to create candles with distinct themes that offer you unique experiences, allowing you to find a cozy and different kind of enjoyment right at home.
