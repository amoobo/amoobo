Welcome to Amoobo

At Amoobo, we believe that warmth is more than just a feeling—it’s something you can hold, smell, and share. Based in Calgary, Canada, our handcrafted scented candles are lovingly made one by one, with care woven into every detail.

Each candle carries a unique fragrance designed to transform your space and evoke moments of comfort, joy, and serenity. Whether you're winding down after a long day or creating a cozy atmosphere for loved ones, Amoobo brings a touch of handmade warmth into your home.
